Site Name:

Splashy Dashiez

Site Purpose:

For a car wash company to provide different types of car wash starting from a basic to a premium type. The site will help provide details why you should go to this car wash.

Scenarios:

1. What type of washes do they provide?

2. What are some pricings for each washes?

Color Schema & Typography:

A simple, clean design will be used with a primary color of a dark slate blue (#2c3e50) on a light grey background (#f4f4f9). Text will be a readable sans-serif like Roboto.
